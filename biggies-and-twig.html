Biggies and Twig and the Truth About Lies

By Taylor Lawhon

About the Book

A witty, rhyming adventure through the land of half-truths, marketing tricks, emotional appeals, and fake science. When Twig—a curious pup with a nose for sniffing out nonsense—meets Biggies, a toy come to life, the two tumble into a world where information isn’t always what it seems.

Why It Matters

This book teaches kids ages 5–9 how to think critically about the messages they hear—from ads to headlines to peer pressure. It covers emotional, logical, and scientific deception in a kid-friendly, hilarious way.

Inside You'll Find:

  • Catchy AABB rhymes for easy reading aloud
  • Visual metaphors like “The Forest of Feelings” and “The Study Swamp”
  • Memorable characters that spark discussion at home or in the classroom
