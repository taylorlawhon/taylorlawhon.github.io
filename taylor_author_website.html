About Taylor

Taylor Lawhon writes picture books that speak to the sensitive, curious, and wide-eyed children of the world. Their stories blend cozy surrealism, emotional literacy, and philosophical wonder—designed to resonate with both kids and the adults who read to them. Taylor’s books are especially beloved in homeschool and gifted communities.
